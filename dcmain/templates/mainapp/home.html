This webpage illustrates minimal usage of a Debts & Credits Recording module, which is part of a larger bookkeeping app, Tradepally. {# The module is used to keep simple but detailed and dynamic record of debtors and creditors for individual, business, or organisation.#} I recently exposed the module for use in another project via an API and decided to put up a glympse here. The module's full functionality includes more indebt operations, like: linking debtor to creditor and vice versa, and linking either to other users on the platform who can assent to txns meant for them, thereby adding a layer of legitimacy, among other features. The complete code for this project is at This GitHub Repo.

Transactions

There can be Debt, Credit, Debt Payment, and Credit Payment transactions. A debt txn represents money you received from a creditor and credit txn represents money you gave to a debtor. And on the flip side, a debt payment txn reps payment you made to a creditor and credit payment reps money a debtor paid to you.
